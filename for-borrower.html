For Borrowers

Lending Star is an Internet P2P-lending platform through which clients who need to borrow funds search and attract investors to finance their personal or business needs. Lending Star Internet-platform provides customers with a complete service of implementation of P2P-lending scheme: from your idea to attract funding to attracting investors for your loan and its subsequent repayment.

Lending Star services for borrowers:

  • Due diligence and consultation of the borrower’s financial capacity in terms of attracting investment / obtaining a loan.
  • Access to a personal account, through which you can manage your obligations, track payments, and attract new financing in real time.
  • Assistance with application creation and placement for funding on the Lending Star platform. Selection of investors for financing your project.
  • Implementation of bidding between the borrower and potential investors, regarding financing conditions.
  • Complete organization of concluding transactions with investors over the Internet to attract financing.
  • Deal support from the moment of financing up to the full repayment of the loan: account receivable, payment processing, and possible renegotiation.

Peer-2-peer financing scheme implies straightforward / direct ("person-to-person") loans from investor to borrower without using traditional financial institutions such as banks, finance companies, and pawnshops. Borrowers search for investors to finance their needs or the needs of their business through specialized Internet service.

An important point is that under the loan money is transferred directly (without crediting Lending Star account or an account of any other party) from investor's account to borrower’s account and back, which ensures high level of safety of customer funds during implementation of the financing process.
